What is SSO ID Rajasthan?

SSO ID Rajasthan (Single Sign-On) is an integrated digital platform launched by the Government of Rajasthan. It allows citizens, businesses, and government employees to access multiple government services using a single login ID and password.

With one SSO ID, users can apply for schemes, check application status, access job portals, pay utility bills, and use various online government services securely.

Services Available on SSO Portal

  • e-Mitra Services
  • Rajasthan Government Job Applications
  • Bhamashah & Jan Aadhaar Services
  • Electricity & Water Bill Payment
  • Scholarship Applications
  • Business Registrations & Licenses
  • Employee & Department Services

SSO Login & Registration

SSO Login

Login using your registered SSO ID and password to access all Rajasthan government services.


SSO Registration

New users can register using Jan Aadhaar, Google, Facebook, or Bhamashah ID.


Help & Support

If you face any issues while logging in or using services, you can contact SSO Rajasthan Helpdesk or visit your nearest e-Mitra center.
